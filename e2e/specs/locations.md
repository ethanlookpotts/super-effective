## Location list renders

1. Go to the app
2. Click the "WHERE AM I" tab
3. Expect: "Viridian Forest" location card is visible
4. Expect: "Safari Zone" location card is visible

## TMs & HMs accordion is present

1. Go to the app
2. Click the "WHERE AM I" tab
3. Expect: a card with text "TMS & HMS" is visible

## TM search by move name

1. Go to the app
2. Click the "WHERE AM I" tab
3. Type "earthquake" into the search field
4. Expect: a TM card with "TM26" and "Earthquake" is visible
5. Expect: the location shows "Viridian Gym"

## TM search by TM number

1. Go to the app

3. Type "tm26" into the search field
4. Expect: a TM card with "Earthquake" is visible
